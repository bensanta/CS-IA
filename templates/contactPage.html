About Magnolia Saloon

Hi, I'm Adrienn Sánta, founder of Magnolia Saloon. My passion for holistic wellness was born from a personal health journey, leading me to become a certified aromatherapist and massage therapist. At Magnolia Saloon, I specialize in ZYTO biocommunication scanning and carefully selected DoTerra essential oils, ensuring a unique, personalized wellness experience for each client.

My approach combines the healing power of touch with the therapeutic benefits of nature's remedies. I've created not just a place for treatments, but a sanctuary for mind, body, and spirit. At Magnolia Saloon, every visit is an opportunity for transformation and rejuvenation. Join me in this journey towards holistic well-being.
